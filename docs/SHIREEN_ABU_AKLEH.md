# Shireen Abu Akleh: The Palestinian Voice

> A symbol of press freedom — killed while simply doing her job.

---

## The Journalist

Shireen Abu Akleh (1971-2022) was a Palestinian-American journalist who spent 25 years covering the Israeli-Palestinian conflict for Al Jazeera.

---

## Her Story

### Early Career
- Born in Jerusalem
- Started at Al Jazeera in 1997
- Became one of the most recognized voices covering Israel-Palestine

### What She Covered
- The Second Intifada (2000-2005)
- Multiple wars and conflicts
- The everyday lives of Palestinians
- Israeli military operations

### Her Reputation
- Known for her calm, professional reporting
- Trusted by both Palestinians and international audiences
- Covering one of the most dangerous conflicts in the world

---

## The Killing

### May 11, 2022
- Covering an Israeli raid in Jenin refugee camp, West Bank
- Wearing a blue PRESS vest (clearly marked)
- Shot in the head by Israeli forces
- Died shortly after

### The Investigation
- Multiple investigations concluded Israeli fire killed her
- US FBI launched investigation
- Footage showed she was clearly marked as press

---

## Legacy

- Became a symbol of press freedom worldwide
- Palestinian-American citizen
- Her death was condemned globally
- The phrase "Shireen" became synonymous with journalist safety
