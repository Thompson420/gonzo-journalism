# Tom Wolfe: The New Journalism Pioneer

> He helped invent the style that became gonzo.

---

## The Writer

Tom Wolfe (1930-2018) was a key figure in the New Journalism movement.

---

## His Work

### Key Books
- "The Kandy" (1965)
- "The Right Stuff" (1979)
- "Bonfire of the Vanities" (1987)

### Style
- Immersive reporting
- First person
- Detailed scenes
- "New Journalism"

---

## Legacy

Tom Wolfe represents:
- The New Journalism movement
- Influencing gonzo
- Long-form immersive writing
