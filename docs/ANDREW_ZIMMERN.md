# Andrew Zimmern

## Overview
Andrew Zimmern is an American food and travel writer known for his explorations of unusual cuisines.

## Biography
- **Born:** July 17, 1962, New York City
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Zimmern's career includes restaurant criticism, food writing, and television presenting.

## Notable Works
- "Bizarre Foods" series
- "Andrew Zimmern's Field Guide"
- "The Food Lover's Guide to"

## Gonzo Connection
Zimmern's willingness to try any food, anywhere, reflects the gonzo spirit of immersive experience.

## Legacy
One of the most recognizable food travel personalities.
