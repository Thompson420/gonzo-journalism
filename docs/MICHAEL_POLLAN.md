# Michael Pollan

## Overview
Michael Pollan is an American author known for his explorations of food systems and the relationship between humans and nature.

## Biography
- **Born:** March 6, 1955, Poughkeepsie, New York
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Pollan has written extensively about food, agriculture, and the environment.

## Notable Works
- "The Omnivore's Dilemma" (2006)
- "Cooked" (2013)
- "In Defense of Food" (2008)

## Gonzo Connection
Pollan's immersive investigations into food systems bring gonzo storytelling to food journalism.

## Legacy
One of the most influential food writers of our time.
