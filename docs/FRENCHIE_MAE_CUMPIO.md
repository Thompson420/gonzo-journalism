# Frenchie Mae Cumpio: The Filipino Prisoner

> She was 21. They jailed her. She's still there.

---

## The Journalist

Frenchie Mae Cumpio is a Filipino journalist who has been imprisoned since 2020 — over 6 years without trial.

---

## Her Story

### Early Life
- Born 1999
- Became executive director of a local news website
- Age 20-21

### The Investigation
- Exposed army harassment of rural farmers
- Documented militarization
- Told the truth about conditions in the Philippines

---

## The Imprisonment

### 2020
- ARRESTED
- WEAPONS PLANTED ON HER
- Grenade and firearm "found" in her possession
- Age 21

### The Trial
- STILL WAITING
- Over 6 years in prison WITHOUT TRIAL
- Faces 40 YEARS if convicted
- International condemnation

### Who Supports Her
- Reporters Without Borders (RSF)

- International press freedom organizations

## Legacy

Frenchie Mae Cumpio represents:
