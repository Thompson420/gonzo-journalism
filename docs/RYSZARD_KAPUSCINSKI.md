# Ryszard Kapuściński: The Polish Master

> He turned journalism into literature.

---

## The Journalist

Ryszard Kapuściński (1932-2007) was a Polish journalist known for his literary, immersive reporting.

---

## His Work

### Major Books
- "The Emperor" (about Haile Selassie)
- "Shah of Shahs" (about Iranian revolution)
- "The Soccer War"
- "Another Day of Life"

### Style
- Literary gonzo
- Personal immersion
- Poetic prose
- Philosophical depth

---

## What He Covered
- African independence movements
- Iranian revolution
- Latin American politics
- Asian conflicts

---

## Legacy

Kapuściński represents:
- The Polish perspective on world affairs
- Journalism as literature
- The gonzo tradition in Europe
