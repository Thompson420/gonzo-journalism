# Miriam: The Liberian Truth-Seeker

> She reported on the Ebola orphans. They tried to silence her.

---

## The Journalist

Miriam Doe is a Liberian investigative journalist focused on post-war human rights issues.

---

## Her Work

### Key Coverage
- Ebola outbreak child displacement
- Healthcare access in rural areas
- Gender-based violence during crises

### Major Investigations
- Exposed false Ebola death reports to boost military funding
- Documented sexual violence against women in refugee camps
- Uncovered illegal mining operations funding warlords

---

## The Price

### Threats & Confinement
- Multiple death threats after publishing "Missing Billion" investigation
- Former collaborator murdered (Ahmed Washington, 2021)
- Forced exile in 2025 to continue work remotely

---

## Legacy
Miriam represents:
- The power of documenting human rights abuses
- The cost of speaking truth in post-conflict societies
- Women's resilience in conflict zones
