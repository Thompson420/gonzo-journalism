# Nicky Hager: The Kiwi Investigator

> He exposes the secrets that powerful people want hidden.

---

## The Journalist

Nicky Hager is a New Zealand investigative journalist known for his deep investigations into government and corporate secrets.

---

## His Story

### Background
- ICIJ member (International Consortium of Investigative Journalists)
- Based in New Zealand

### Major Investigations

#### "Secret Power" (1996)
- Exposed the Echelon surveillance network
- Western governments spying on citizens
- Groundbreaking at the time

#### "Dirty Politics" (2014)
- Exposed attacks from Prime Minister John Key's office
- Leaked emails showed political attacks
- Caused major political fallout

#### "Hit and Run" (2017)
- Investigated New Zealand war crimes in Afghanistan
- Documented civilian killings
- Exposed military cover-ups
