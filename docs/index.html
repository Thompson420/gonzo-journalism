About The NEON Archive Protocol

The NEON Archive Protocol is a comprehensive database documenting gonzo journalists from around the world and throughout history. From Nellie Bly's 1887 undercover investigations to modern podcast journalists, we've catalogued the pioneers who shaped immersive, first-person journalism.
