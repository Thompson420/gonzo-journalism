# Sarah Koenig

## Overview
Sarah Koenig is an American journalist and producer best known as the creator and host of the podcast "Serial."

## Biography
- **Born:** 1970, New York
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Koenig worked for This American Life before creating "Serial," which revolutionized podcast journalism.

## Notable Works
- "Serial" (2014) - first season
- "Serial" (2015) - second season
- This American Life episodes

## Gonzo Connection
Koenig's first-person, narrative approach to investigative journalism brought gonzo storytelling to the podcast era.

## Legacy
"Serial" was the first podcast to win a Peabody Award and changed the landscape of audio journalism.
