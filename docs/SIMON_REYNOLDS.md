# Simon Reynolds

## Overview
Simon Reynolds is a British music journalist known for his deep coverage of electronic and experimental music.

## Biography
- **Born:** 1963, London
- **Nationality:** British

## Career
Reynolds writes for The Wire and The Guardian, covering electronic music, post-punk, and avant-garde.

## Notable Works
- "Energy Flash: A Journey Through Rave Music" (2012)
- "Retro: The Book" (2006)
- "Bring the Noise" (2017)

## Gonzo Connection
Reynolds brings a first-person, immersive approach to electronic music journalism.

## Legacy
One of the most respected voices in contemporary music criticism.
