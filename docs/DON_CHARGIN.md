# Don Chargin

## Overview
Don Chargin was an American boxing writer and historian known as "The Godfather of Boxing."

## Biography
- **Born:** 1928
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Chargin covered over 200 title fights and was known for his immersive, first-person boxing journalism.

## Notable Works
- Boxing columns in major publications
- "The Fight" oral histories

## Gonzo Connection
Chargin's decades of embedded boxing coverage embodied the gonzo spirit.

## Legacy
Considered one of the most influential boxing writers of the 20th century.
