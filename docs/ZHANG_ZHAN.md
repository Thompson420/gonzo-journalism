# Zhang Zhan: The Hunger Strike Hero

> She went to document COVID in Wuhan. They jailed her for it.

---

## The Journalist

Zhang Zhan is a Chinese citizen journalist who was imprisoned for reporting on the COVID-19 pandemic in Wuhan in early 2020.

---

## Her Story

### Background
- Former lawyer who became a citizen journalist
- Traveled to Wuhan in February 2020
- Wanted to document the early days of the pandemic

### What She Did
- Posted videos and articles about:
  - The initial COVID outbreak
  - Government detentions of reporters
  - Harassment of victims' families
  - The lockdown conditions

### The Disappearance
- "Disappeared" in May 2020
- No contact with family for months

---

## The Imprisonment

### The Charges
- Convicted of "picking quarrels and provoking trouble"
- A vague charge used to silence critics

### In Prison
- Went on HUNGER STRIKE
- Force-fed
- Weight dropped to 37 kg (half her original weight)
- Medical concerns ignored

### Release and Re-Arrest
- Released in May 2024
- Re-detained in August 2024
- Sentenced to 4 MORE years in September 2025

---

## Current Status

As of late 2025, Zhang Zhan is still imprisoned.

---

## Her Legacy

Zhang Zhan represents:
- The extreme cost of citizen journalism in China
- The ongoing crackdown on press freedom
- The courage of those who speak truth to power
