# Raman Pratasevich: The Hijacked Journalist

> They diverted a plane to arrest him.

---

## The Journalist

Raman Pratasevich is a Belarusian journalist who was arrested after a Ryanair flight was diverted to Belarus.

---

## His Work

### What He Did
- Worked for Nexta Telegram channel
- Covered Belarusian protests
- Documented government repression

### The Context
- 2020: Massive protests in Belarus after rigged election
- Government cracked down on journalists
- Pratasevich went into exile

---

## The Hijacking

### May 23, 2021
- Flight from Athens to Vilnius
- Diverted to Minsk
- Belarusian fighter jets escorting
- Reason: "bomb threat" (later proven false)

### The Arrest
- Pratasevich arrested
- His girlfriend also arrested
- Charged with "organizing mass riots"

### The Sentence
- Sentenced to 8 YEARS in prison
- Still incarcerated

---

## Aftermath

### International Outrage
- EU condemned the hijacking
- Banned Belarusian airlines
- Called it "state terrorism"

---

## Legacy

Raman Pratasevich represents:
- The lengths dictatorships will go to
- The new era of journalist kidnapping
- The Belarusian resistance
