# Javier Valdez: The Voice of Mexico

> He wrote about the dead so the world wouldn't forget.

---

## The Journalist

Javier Valdez (1967-2017) was a Mexican journalist who specialized in covering drug cartels and organized crime. He was assassinated in 2017.

---

## His Story

### Career
- Worked for Ríodo Mayor and other publications
- Based in Sinaloa, Mexico — heart of the drug trade
- Covered the cartel wars for over a decade

### What He Exposed
- Drug trafficking routes
- Cartel violence
- The human cost of the drug war
- The names of victims

### His Writing
- Wrote books about the cartel wars
- Documented the daily violence
- Gave voices to the silenced

---

## The Assassination

### May 15, 2017
- Shot and killed in Culiacán, Sinaloa
- Targeted for his work
- One of dozens of journalists killed in Mexico

### The Aftermath
- International condemnation
- His death highlighted the danger for journalists in Mexico

---

## Legacy

Javier Valdez represents:
- The extreme danger of covering cartels
- The importance of continuing to write about the victims
- The cost of journalism in Mexico
