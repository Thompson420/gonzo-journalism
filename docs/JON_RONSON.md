# Jon Ronson

## Overview
Jon Ronson is a British journalist and author known for his gonzo-style explorations of unusual subcultures and fringe figures.

## Biography
- **Born:** July 10, 1965, London
- **Nationality:** British

## Career
Ronson writes for The Guardian and other publications, creating immersive pieces about conspiracy theorists, cult leaders, and fringe personalities.

## Notable Works
- "The Men Who Stare at Goats" (2004)
- "The Psychopath Test" (2011)
- "So You've Been Publicly Shamed" (2015)

## Gonzo Connection
Ronson's first-person, often humorous, immersive reporting style is quintessentially gonzo.

## Legacy
One of Britain's most distinctive voices in gonzo journalism.
