# Graham Hunter

## Overview
Graham Hunter was a British football writer known for his immersive FC Barcelona reporting.

## Biography
- **Died:** January 1, 2017
- **Nationality:** British

## Career
Hunter spent decades covering FC Barcelona, producing in-depth, first-person football journalism.

## Notable Works
- "Barça: The Making of the Greatest Club in the World" (2010)
- "Pep Guardiola: The Making of a Manager" (2018)

## Gonzo Connection
Hunter's immersive, inside-access football writing embodied the gonzo approach.

## Legacy
One of the most respected football writers of his generation.
