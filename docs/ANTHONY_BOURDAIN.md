# Anthony Bourdain

## Overview
Anthony Bourdain was an American chef, author, and television personality who revolutionized food and travel journalism.

## Biography
- **Born:** June 25, 1956, New York City
- **Died:** June 8, 2018, France
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Bourdain's career spanned restaurant cooking, writing, and television presenting. His shows brought viewers into the world of food and culture.

## Notable Works
- "Kitchen Confidential" (2000)
- "A Cook's Tour" (2002)
- "Parts Unknown" (TV series)

## Gonzo Connection
Bourdain's first-person, immersive approach to food and travel journalism embodied the gonzo spirit.

## Legacy
Bourdain's influence on food media and travel journalism remains profound.
