# Rana Ayyub: The Indian Warrior

> She exposed the truth about Gujarat — and paid the price.

---

## The Journalist

Rana Ayyub is an Indian investigative journalist who went undercover to expose the 2002 Gujarat riots cover-up.

---

## Her Story

### Background
- Indian journalist
- Washington Post columnist
- BBC contributor

### The Gujarat Files

In 2018, Rana went UNDERCOVER for 10 months
- Posed as "Maithili Tyagi"
- Investigated the 2002 Gujarat riots
- Exposed how the state covered up the violence

### What She Found
- Evidence of state involvement in the riots
- How victims were silenced
- Political cover-ups at the highest levels

### The Result
- Got Amit Shah (Modi's close ally) briefly jailed
- Exposed the truth

---

## The Price

### Harassment
- Death threats
- DEEPFAKE PORN created of her
- Doxxed — her address published
- Constant online harassment

### Awards
- International awards for courage
- Continues to write despite threats
