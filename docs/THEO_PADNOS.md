# Theo Padnos: The Kidnapped American

> He went undercover in Syria. Then they kidnapped him.

---

## The Journalist

Theo Padnos is an American journalist who was kidnapped and held hostage in Syria for almost two years.

---

## His Story

### Background
- American journalist and author
- Wrote about Islam and the Middle East

### The Undercover Work
- Went undercover as a Muslim
- Book: "Undercover Muslim"
- Explored Islamic communities in the West

---

## The Kidnapping

### 2012
- Went to Syria to cover the war
- Kidnapped by al-Nusra Front (Al-Qaeda in Syria)
- Held for ALMOST TWO YEARS
- Tortured
- Kept in darkness

### The Experience
- Documented his captivity
- Later wrote "Blindfold" about the experience

---

## The Rescue

### 2014
- Released after negotiations
- Returned to the US
- Made documentary: "Theo Who Lived"

---

## Legacy

Theo Padnos represents:
- The extreme dangers of warzone journalism
- Going undercover in hostile territory
- Surviving against the odds
