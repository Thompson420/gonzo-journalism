# Music Gonzo Journalists

## Introduction
Music journalism has been transformed by gonzo-style writing - immersive, first-person accounts of concerts, backstage access, and the wild world of rock, punk, and beyond. From Lester Bangs to modern music critics, these writers bring readers into the mosh pit.
