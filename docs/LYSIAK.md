# Lysiak: The Polish Investigative Reporter

> He exposed the communist regime. Then returned to uncover its shadow.

---

## The Journalist

Ryszard Lisiak (1931-2022) was a Polish investigative journalist and political figure who played a pivotal role in exposing communist-era corruption in Poland.

---

## His Story

### Early Career
- Journalist and member of the Polish Socialist Party
- Worked for "Polityka" and "Gazeta Wyborcza"

### The Investigations
- Exposed communist-era human rights abuses
- Documented secret police informants
- Reclaimed property stolen during socialist era

### The Communist Trials
- Arrested in 1981 during martial law
- Charged with "seditious act"
- Imprisoned for 8 months
- International human rights protests ensued

### Return to Investigative Work
- After 1989, continued investigating post-communist corruption
- Founded the "Polish Institute of Investigative Journalism"

---

## Legacy

- Helped establish Poland's anti-corruption framework
- Mentor to younger investigative journalists
- Symbol of intellectual resistance to authoritarianism
