# Lester Bangs

## Overview
Lester Bangs was an American music journalist who helped define rock criticism and is considered a pioneer of music gonzo journalism.

## Biography
- **Born:** March 14, 1948, California
- **Died:** April 30, 1982, New York City
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Bangs wrote for Creem magazine and is credited with inventing the rock critic persona.

## Notable Works
- "Psychotic Reactions and Carburetor Dung" (1987)
- Creem magazine columns

## Gonzo Connection
Bangs was one of the first to bring gonzo-style first-person writing to music journalism.

## Legacy
Considered the father of rock music criticism.
