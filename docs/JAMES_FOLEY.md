# James Foley

## Overview
James Foley was an American journalist and video journalist who documented conflicts in Libya and Syria.

## Biography
- **Born:** October 18, 1973, Chicago, Illinois
- **Died:** September 2, 2014, Syria
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Foley worked as a conflict journalist, primarily for GlobalPost, covering revolutions and wars in Libya and Syria.

## Notable Works
- Libya conflict coverage (2011)
- Syria conflict reporting (2012-2014)
- Video journalism for GlobalPost

## Gonzo Connection
Foley's immersive, on-the-ground conflict reporting represented the gonzo tradition of placing oneself in the heart of the action.

## Legacy
His death at the hands of ISIS brought international attention to the dangers faced by journalists in conflict zones.
