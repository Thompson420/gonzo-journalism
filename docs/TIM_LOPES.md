# Tim Lopes: The Brazilian Legend

> He went into the favelas. They killed him for it.

---

## The Journalist

Tim Lopes was a Brazilian TV Globo investigative journalist who was murdered while investigating drug trafficking and child prostitution in Rio de Janeiro.

---

## His Story

### Career
- Worked for TV Globo
- One of Brazil's top investigative journalists
- Known for going undercover

### The Investigation
- Went into Rio favelas
- Investigating drug trafficking
- Investigating child prostitution
- Hidden camera, documenting everything

---

## The End

### 2002
- Kidnapped by drug traffickers
- TORTURED
- EXECUTED
- Body BURNED in secret grave
- Found 41 bone fragments

---

## Legacy

Tim Lopes represents:
- The extreme danger in Latin America
- Going where other won't
- The cost of journalism

### Aftermath
- His death led to increased protections
- Became a symbol of journalist danger
- International outrage
