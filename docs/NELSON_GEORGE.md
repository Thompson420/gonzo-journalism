# Nelson George

## Overview
Nelson George is an American writer and filmmaker known for chronicling hip hop and urban culture.

## Biography
- **Born:** 1957, Brooklyn New York
- **Nationality:** American

## Career,
George has written extensively about hip hop, soul, and African American culture.

## Notable Works
- "The Hip Hop Years" (1999)
- "Death of the Color Bar" (1992)
- "Urban Romance" (2015)

## Gonzo Connection
George's immersive coverage of hip hop culture brought gonzo journalism to urban music.

## Legacy
Considered one of the most important chroniclers of hip hop culture.
