# Gabriel García Márquez: The Gonzo Influence

> He brought magical realism to the world. But his journalism was pure gonzo.

---

## The Writer

Gabriel García Márquez (1927-2014) was a Colombian novelist and journalist.

---

## His Work

### Literature
- "One Hundred Years of Solitude"
- "Love in the Time of Cholera"
- Nobel Prize in Literature (1982)

### Journalism
- Wrote for El Espectador
- Documented Colombian reality
- Used novelistic techniques in journalism

---

## The Gonzo Connection

### Immersive
- Lived the stories
- Embedded in Colombian life
- Personal, subjective

### Style
- Called "magical realism"
- Blended fiction and nonfiction
- Influenced gonzo journalism

---

## Legacy

García Márquez influenced:
- Latin American literature
- Gonzo journalism
- The blending of fiction and nonfiction
