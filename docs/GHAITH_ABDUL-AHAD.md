# Ghaith Abdul-Ahad: The Iraqi Gonzo

> He escaped Saddam's army and spent years documenting war.

---

## The Journalist

Ghaith Abdul-Ahad is an Iraqi journalist who has covered wars and conflicts across the Middle East. He goes where others won't.

---

## His Story

### Early Life
- Born in Baghdad, 1975
- Deserted Saddam Hussein's army at 17
- Lived underground for 6 years

### The Escape
- Fled Iraq
- Became a journalist
- Started documenting the chaos

---

## His Work

### Iraq War
- Embedded with US forces
- Documented the chaos and violence
- Exposed what was happening on the ground

### Other Conflicts
- Somalia
- Sudan
- Afghanistan
- Libya
- Syria

---

## The Dangers

### Taliban Imprisonment (2010)
- Held by Taliban for 5 days
- Documented his experience

### Libyan Detention (2011)
- Detained by Libyan Army
- Rough treatment
