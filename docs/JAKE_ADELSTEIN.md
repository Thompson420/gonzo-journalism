# Jake Adelstein: Tokyo Vice

> He went inside the Yakuza. And lived to tell about it.

---

## The Journalist

Jake Adelstein is an American journalist who spent years investigating the Japanese organized crime (Yakuza).

---

## His Story

### Background
- American journalist in Japan
- Worked for the Tokyo Vice newspaper
- First American to work as a crime reporter in Japan

### Tokyo Vice
- Exposed the Yakuza (Japanese mafia)
- Documented their connections to business and politics
- Went where other journalists wouldn't

### The Book
- "Tokyo Vice: Reporting from the Dark Side of Japan"
- Detailed his experiences
- Exposed the hidden world

---

## The Price

### Death Threats
- Constant threats from Yakuza
- Had to leave Japan
- Lives with protection

### Aftermath
- The book became a hit
- TV series adaptation
- Continued writing about organized crime

---

## Legacy

Jake Adelstein represents:
- Going deep into organized crime
- The American gonzo tradition
- Exposing what's hidden
