# Ján Kuciak: The Spark That Shook Slovakia

> He was just 27 years old when he was murdered. His death triggered the largest protests in Slovak history.

---

## The Story

Ján Kuciak (1987-2018) was a 27-year-old investigative journalist at Aktuality.sk who was murdered alongside his girlfriend Martina Kušnírová.

---

## His Work

### What He Investigated
- Government corruption in Slovakia
- Links between Italian Mafia ('Ndrangheta) and Slovak politicians
- Businessman Marian Kočner and his suspected connections to organized crime

### The Investigation That Killed Him
His final investigation was into:
- How Italian Mafia (Ndrangheta) had infiltrated Slovak businesses
- Suspected links between government officials and organized crime

---

## The Murder

**February 21, 2018:**
- Shot and killed in his home in Veľká Mača
- His girlfriend Martina (also 26) was killed with him
- Both were shot in the head

---

## The Aftermath

### Public Outrage
His murder triggered:
- **Largest protests in Slovakia since the Velvet Revolution**
- Calls for Prime Minister Robert Fico to resign
- International condemnation

### The Investigation
- 2019: Businessman Marian Kočner arrested
- 2020: Alleged hitman arrested
- Case is still ongoing

### Political Fallout
- Prime Minister Robert Fico resigned (partly due to pressure from protests)
- Slovakia's government became more unstable

---

## Legacy

Ján Kuciak's murder became a symbol of:
- The danger facing journalists in Eastern Europe
- The need for EU-wide protection of press freedom
- The connections between organized crime and politics
