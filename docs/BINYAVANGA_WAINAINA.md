## The Journalist

Binyavanga Wainaina (1971-2019) was a Kenyan writer who changed African literature.

## His Story

### Early Life
- Born in Nakuru, Kenya
- Dropped out of university in South Africa
- Worked for dad's cotton business

### Kwani?
- Won Caine Prize 2002
- Used £12,000 to found Kwani? magazine
- Kenya's literary revolution
