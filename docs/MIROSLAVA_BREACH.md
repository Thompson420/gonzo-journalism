# Miroslava Breach: Voice of Chihuahua

> She was killed for doing what journalists do — telling the truth.

---

## The Journalist

Miroslava Breach Velducea (1962-2017) was a Mexican investigative journalist who spent decades exposing the connections between organized crime and politics in Chihuahua, Mexico.

---

## Her Story

### Early Career
- Worked for El Diario de Chihuahua and later La Jornada
- Specialized in covering drug cartels, corruption, and human rights

### What She Exposed
- Drug cartel infiltration in Chihuahua politics
- Links between criminal organizations and local politicians
- Human rights violations in the Sierra Tarahumara region
- Female homicides in Ciudad Juárez

### Notable Investigations
- Exposed how drug cartels had infiltrated local governments
- Documented connections between politicians and the Sinaloa Cartel
- Investigated corruption of former Governor César Duarte

---

## The Threats

Throughout her career, Miroslava faced:
- Death threats from cartels
- Intimidation from politicians
- Warnings from colleagues that her life was in danger

---

## The Assassination

### March 23, 2017
- Shot 8 times in her car
- Killed while driving her son to school
- Attack was carried out in broad daylight

### The Aftermath
- Multiple suspects arrested
- Cartel-linked killers
- Her murder is still not fully resolved

---

## Legacy

Miroslava Breach became a SYMBOL of:
- The dangers facing journalists in Mexico
- The need for protection of investigative reporters
- The ongoing crisis of journalist murders in Latin America
