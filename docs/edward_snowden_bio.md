# Edward Snowden - The Whistleblower

## Full Biography

**Edward Joseph Snowden** was born on June 21, 1983, in Elizabeth City, North Carolina. He is a former CIA employee and National Security Agency (NSA) contractor who leaked classified information about mass surveillance programs in 2013.

## Early Life

- Born in Elizabeth City, North Carolina
- Father: Coastal Guard officer
- Mother: Computer consultant
- Learned computers early
- Never graduated from high school (got GED)
- Attended Anne Arundel Community College

## Career

### Early Work (2005-2013)
- CIA: IT security
- NSA: System administrator
- Worked for various government contractors
- Had top secret clearance

### The Realizations
- Became disillusioned with mass surveillance
- "I didn't want to live in a world where everything is recorded"
- "The NSA is building a system that has the capability to record everything"

## The Leaks

### June 2013: The Revelation
- Copied thousands of classified documents
- Left Hawaii for Hong Kong
- Met journalists: Glenn Greenwald, Laura Poitras
- Released first documents: June 5, 2013

### What Was Exposed

1. **PRISM**
   - Direct access to Google, Facebook, Apple servers
   - Tech giants complicit

2. **Phone Records**
   - NSA collecting metadata on millions
   - Verizon court order revealed

3. **XKeyscore**
   - Program that searches "nearly everything a user does on the internet"

4. **Upstream Collection**
   - Tapping undersea cables
   - Collecting data as it flows

5. **Stellarwind**
   - Warrantless wiretapping since 2001

## Aftermath

### Flight
- Hong Kong → Moscow (June 23, 2013)
- US passport revoked
- Stuck in Russia (since 2013)
- Temporary asylum (2013-2022)
- Permanent residency (2022)

### Legal Status
- Charged with espionage (2013)
- US revoked passport (in Russia)
- Would face 30 years if returns
- Possible exchange for prisoners

## What He Leaked

### 1.7 Million Documents
- 200+ intelligence briefings
- Programs in 60+ countries
- Revealed mass surveillance globally
- Changed global conversation

## Impact

### The "Snowden Effect"
- Global encryption adoption
- Privacy awareness increased
- Tech companies improved security
- GDPR in Europe
- USA Freedom Act (2015)

## Awards & Recognition

## Life in Russia
