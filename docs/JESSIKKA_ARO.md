# Jessikka Aro: The Finnish Truth-Seeker

> She investigated Russian trolls. They jailed her for it.

---

## The Journalist

Jessikka Aro is a Finnish investigative journalist who exposed Russian disinformation campaigns.

---

## Her Story

### Background
- Finnish journalist
- Works for Helsingin Sanomat
- Specialized in Russian disinformation

### What She Exposed
- Russian troll farms
- Disinformation campaigns targeting Finland
- Pro-Russian propaganda networks

### The Investigation
- Went UNDERCOVER
- Documented how Russia spreads misinformation
- Exposed the networks

---

## The Price

### Arrest and Conviction
- JAILED in 2019
- Convicted of revealing classified information
- First Finnish journalist jailed for investigation

### Aftermath
- International support
- Raised questions about press freedom
- Case used as warning to other journalists
