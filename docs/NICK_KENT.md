# Nick Kent

## Overview
Nick Kent is a British music journalist known for his decades of rock and punk coverage.

## Biography
- **Born:** 1951, London
- **Nationality:** British

## Career
Kent wrote for NME and other publications, documenting the punk and new wave movements.

## Notable Works
- "The Dark Stuff" (1996)
- NME articles

## Gonzo Connection
Kent's immersive rock journalism embodied the gonzo spirit.

## Legacy
One of Britain's most respected music journalists.
