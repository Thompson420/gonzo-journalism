# John Pilger: The Investigative Legend

> He took on empires. And never backed down.

---

## The Journalist

John Pilger (1942-2023) was an Australian-British investigative journalist and documentary filmmaker.

---

## His Work

### Major Investigations
- Vietnam War exposés
- Exposed secret US wars in Cambodia
- Documented the breakup of Yugoslavia
- Investigated Australian government corruption

### Documentary Films
- "The Quiet Invasion"
- "The New Rulers of the World"
- "Year of Living Dangerously"

---

## The Price

### Threats and Censorship
- Faced press censorship in Australia and UK
- Legal threats from powerful interests
- Permanent exile from mainstream media

---

## Legacy

John Pilger represents:
- The fearless truth-teller
- The power of muckraking
- International solidarity
