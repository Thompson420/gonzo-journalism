# Seymour Hersh: The Legend

> He's been breaking the biggest stories for over 50 years.

---

## The Journalist

Seymour Hersh is one of the most famous investigative journalists in the world. At 80+ years old, he's still breaking major stories.

---

## His Story

### Early Career
- Started at the Chicago Sun
- Joined the New York Times in 1971

### The Big Breaks

#### My Lai Massacre (1969)
- Exposed the My Lai massacre in Vietnam
- American soldiers killed hundreds of Vietnamese civilians
- One of the most important journalism stories of the 20th century
- Won Pulitzer Prize (1970)

#### Abu Ghraib (2004)
- Exposed the torture scandal at Abu Ghraib prison in Iraq
- Photos shocked the world
- Documented US government abuse

#### Syrian Chemical Weapons (2013)
- Exposed Syrian government's use of chemical weapons
- Contradicted US government claims

#### And many more...
- Pentagon Papers
- CIA surveillance
- Nixon administration scandals

## His Method

- RELentless: Follows stories for years

- COURAGE: Publishes regardless of pressure
