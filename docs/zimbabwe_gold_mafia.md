# Zimbabwe Gold Mafia - Expanded Individual Profiles

## Background
The "Zimbabwe Gold Mafia" investigation was a 2023 Al Jazeera documentary series exposing a gold smuggling and money laundering network involving Zimbabwean government officials. Multiple journalists worked on this investigation.

---

### 1. Hamza Mohamed
- **Nationality:** Kenyan/Somali
- **Era:** 2020s
- **Contribution:** Investigative journalist
- **Archive Status:** Al Jazeera archives
- **Notes:** Lead investigator on Gold Mafia series; went undercover exposing gold smuggling routes; documented money laundering through Dubai; faced threats from smuggling networks

### 2. Iqbal Survé
- **Nationality:** South African
- **Era:** 2020s
- **Contribution:** Financial journalist
- **Archive Status:** International archives
- **Notes:** Investigated money laundering connections to South African banks; documented how gold proceeds were laundered through legitimate businesses; contributed financial analysis to Gold Mafia series

### 3. Simba Chitembwe
- **Nationality:** Zimbabwean
- **Era:** 2020s
- **Contribution:** Investigative journalist
- **Archive Status:** Zimbabwean archives
- **Notes:** Local Zimbabwean researcher for Gold Mafia series; documented connections between ZANU-PF officials and gold smugglers; went into hiding after exposé; faces ongoing threats

### 4. Angelo Izama
- **Nationality:** Ugandan
- **Era:** 2010s-20s
- **Contribution:** Investigative journalist
- **Archive Status:** International archives
- **Notes:** Investigated gold smuggling routes through East Africa; documented links between Zimbabwe gold and conflict minerals in DRC; contributed regional analysis to series
