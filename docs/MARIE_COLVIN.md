# Marie Colvin

## Overview
Marie Colvin was an American journalist who covered humanitarian crises and conflicts around the world.

## Biography
- **Born:** December 12, 1957, New York City
- **Died:** February 22, 2012, Homs, Syria
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Colvin worked for The Sunday Times for decades, covering conflicts in Chechnya, Kosovo, Sri Lanka, Syria, and more.

## Notable Works
- Extensive Sunday Times reporting
- Covered Siege of Sarajevo
- Documented Chechen war crimes

## Gonzo Connection
Colvin's firsthand, immersive war reporting embodied the gonzo spirit of placing oneself at the center of the story.

## Legacy
Her death in Syria made her a symbol of the risks faced by war journalists. She was nominated for the Nobel Peace Prize.
