# Valentin Gendrot: The French Undercover

> He went undercover with the Paris police. What he found was shocking.

---

## The Journalist

Valentin Gendrot is a French investigative journalist who went undercover with the Paris police force for 6 months.

---

## The Investigation

### What He Did
- Got a job as a police officer
- Went undercover in Parisian police stations
- Documented what he saw

### What He Found
- Police brutality
- Systemic racism
- Fabricated evidence
- Violence against suspects

### The Book: "Flic" (2020)
- Exposed the dark side of French policing
- Shocked France
- Led to investigations

---

## The Price

### Police Backlash
- Faced threats from colleagues
- Had to leave France
- Constant security concerns

---

## Legacy

Valentin Gendrot represents:
- Going undercover in your own country
- Exposing police misconduct
- The French gonzo tradition
