# Wright Thompson

## Overview
Wright Thompson is an American sportswriter known for his long-form, immersive ESPN features.

## Biography
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Thompson writes long-form sports features for ESPN, known for deep dives into sports culture.

## Notable Works
- "The Cost of Playing Games"
- Various ESPN long-form articles

## Gonzo Connection
Thompson's immersive, first-person sports writing embodies the gonzo tradition.

## Legacy
One of ESPN's most acclaimed long-form writers.
