# Mark Kurlansky

## Overview
Mark Kurlansky is an American writer known for his gonzo-style explorations of food history and culture.

## Biography
- **Born:** November 12, 1940, New York City
- **Nationality:** American

## Career
Kurlansky writes about food, history, and culture with an immersive, first-person approach.

## Notable Works
- "Salt: A World History" (2002)
- "Cod: A Biography of the Fish That Changed the World" (1997)
- "The Big Oyster" (2006)

## Gonzo Connection
Kurlansky's first-person explorations of food history embody the gonzo approach.

## Legacy
His books have helped popularize food history as a genre.
