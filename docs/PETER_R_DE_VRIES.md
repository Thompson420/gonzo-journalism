> "I have no intention of stopping."

## The Crime Journalist

Peter R. de Vries (1956-2021) was a legendary Dutch crime journalist who became a household name in the Netherlands for his decades of work exposing organized crime.

---

## His Story

### Career
- Started in journalism in the 1980s
- Became famous for covering high-profile crime cases
- Developed relationships with both criminals and police

### Notable Cases
- **Freddy Heineken kidnapping** (1983) — Helped solve the case
- **Natalee Holloway disappearance** — Emmy Award winning documentary
- **Ridouan Taghi case** — Advising witness in major drug lord trial

### His Methods
- Known for going to dangerous places
- Built trust with criminals and victims alike
- Not afraid to show the human side of crime stories

---

## The Attack

### July 6, 2021
- Shot 5 times in Amsterdam
- Died 9 days later
- Attack was clearly targeted

### His Legacy
- Called "a national hero" by Amsterdam mayor
- King Willem-Alexander expressed shock
- Attack condemned by EU President
