# Omar Radi: The Moroccan Target

> They jailed him. They spied on him. He still speaks truth.

## The Journalist

Omar Radi is a Moroccan investigative journalist who has been imprisoned and targeted with spyware.

---

## His Work

### What He Investigated
- Corruption in Morocco
- Human rights violations
- Political issues

### His Reporting
- Worked for various Moroccan publications
- Critical of the government

---

## The Price

- JAILED in 2020
- Convicted of "espionage"
- Released in 2024 after royal pardon

### Surveillance
- Targeted with NSO Group's PEGASUS spyware
- Phone infected with sophisticated malware
- International condemnation

## Aftermath

- Still faces challenges
- International support
