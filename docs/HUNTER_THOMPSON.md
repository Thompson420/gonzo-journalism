# Hunter S. Thompson: The Father of Gonzo

> He invented the word. He lived the life.

---

## The Legend

Hunter S. Thompson (1937-2005) coined the term "gonzo journalism" and became its most famous practitioner.

---

## His Story

### Early Career
- Started as a sports writer
- Worked for various publications

### The Invention
- "The Kentucky Derby Is Decadent and Depraved" (1970)
- "Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas" (1971)

### The Method
- First person
- Immersive
- Participant not observer
- No "objective" distance

---

## The Legacy

- Coined "gonzo journalism"
- Fear and Loathing
- Ran for sheriff of Colorado
- Changed journalism forever
