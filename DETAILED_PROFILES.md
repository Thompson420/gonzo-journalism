## The Fallen Legends

### 🇲🇹 Daphne Caruana Galizia (Malta, 1964-2017)

**The One-Woman WikiLeaks**

Daphne Caruana Galizia was Malta's most fearless investigative journalist. She spent three decades exposing corruption at the highest levels of government, focusing on:
- The Panama Papers scandal in Malta
- Money laundering links between Maltese politicians and organized crime
- Government corruption, nepotism, and patronage

**The Story:**
- Started as a newspaper reporter in 1987
- Launched her blog "Running Commentary" in 2008 — it became Malta's most read website
- Broke the Panama Papers story in Malta, revealing government ministers had secret offshore companies
- Faced 48 libel lawsuits
- Her house was firebombed TWICE, her dog was killed, she was arrested for "breaking political silence"

**The End:**
On October 16, 2017, a CAR BOMB exploded her car near her home. She was 53.

Her last blog post: *"There are crooks everywhere you look now. The situation is desperate."*

**Legacy:**
- The Daphne Project: 45 journalists from 18 countries continued her work
- European Parliament renamed their press room in her honor
- Her assassination sparked mass protests leading to Malta's Prime Minister resigning
- The Daphne Caruana Galizia Prize for Journalism was established

**Related Journalists:**
- [Ján Kuciak](#-jan-kuciak-slovakia-1987-2018) — Similar story, similar fate
- [Miroslava Breach](#-miroslava-breach-mexico-1962-2017) — Also assassinated

### 🇸🇰 Ján Kuciak (Slovakia, 1987-2018)

**The Spark That Shook Slovakia**

Ján Kuciak was a 27-year-old investigative journalist at Aktuality.sk when he was murdered.

**The Story:**
- Investigated Slovak government corruption
- Exposed links between Italian Mafia (Ndrangheta) and Slovak politicians
- Had been investigating a businessman with suspected ties to the 'Ndrangheta

**The End:**
February 21, 2018 — Shot and killed in his home. His girlfriend Martina Kušnírová was also killed.

**Legacy:**
- His murder triggered the largest protests in Slovak history
- Led to the resignation of Slovakia's Prime Minister Robert Fico
- The case is still being fought in court

**Related Journalists:**
- [Daphne Caruana Galizia](#-daphne-caruana-galizia-malta-1964-2017) — Both assassinated, both fighting corruption

### 🇲🇽 Miroslava Breach (Mexico, 1962-2017)

**Voice of Chihuahua**

Miroslava Breach Velducea was a legendary investigative journalist in one of the world's most dangerous countries for the press.

**The Story:**
- Exposed drug cartel infiltration in Chihuahua politics
- Investigated human rights violations in the Sierra Tarahumara region
- Documented female homicides in Ciudad Juárez
- Exposed corruption by former Governor César Duarte (900 million pesos embezzled)

**The End:**
March 23, 2017 — Shot 8 times in her car while driving her son to school.

The killers were never fully brought to justice.

**Related Journalists:**
- [Tim Lopes](#-tim-lopes-brazil-?-2002) — Also killed investigating drug-related crimes

## The Ones Who Survived (But Paid a Price)

### 🇨🇳 Zhang Zhan (China, ~1980s-present)

**The Hunger Strike Hero**

A former lawyer who became a citizen journalist.

**The Story:**
- Traveled to Wuhan in February 2020 to document the early COVID-19 pandemic
- Posted on social media about government detentions of reporters and harassment of victims' families
- "Disappeared" in May 2020

**Imprisoned:**
- Convicted of "picking quarrels and provoking trouble" — a vague catch-all charge
- Went on hunger strike in prison
- Force-fed, weight dropped to 37kg (half her original weight)
- Released May 2024 after 4 years
- Re-detained 4 months later (August 2024)
- Sentenced to 4 MORE years in September 2025

**Current Status:** Still imprisoned (as of late 2025)

**Related Journalists:**
- [Frenchie Mae Cumpio](#-frenchie-mae-cumpio-philippines-1999-present) — Also jailed, also imprisoned without trial

### 🇵🇭 Frenchie Mae Cumpio (Philippines, 1999-present)

**The 21-Year-Old Behind Bars**

At just 21, Frenchie Mae Cumpio was already executive director of a local news website.

**The Story:**
- Investigated army harassment of rural farmers in the Eastern Visayas region
- Exposed militarization forcing indigenous communities from their lands

**Imprisoned:**
- Arrested February 2020 (age 21)
- Weapons PLANTED on her (grenade, firearm)
- Still in prison WITHOUT TRIAL as of 2026 — over 6 years
- Faces up to 40 years if convicted
- Case condemned by RSF, UN, and international press freedom organizations

**Related Journalists:**
- [Zhang Zhan](#-zhang-zhan-china-1980s-present) — Also jailed, also refused to be silent

## The Undercover Masters

### 🇩🇰 Mads Brügger (Denmark, 1972-present)

**The Gonzo Machine**

Mads Brügger is the definition of modern gonzo — he doesn't just report the news, he BECOMES the story.

**Major Investigations:**

| Year | Title | Story |
|------|-------|-------|
| 2010 | The Red Chapel | Pretended to be a manager of two Korean comedians touring North Korea. Got INSIDE the hermit kingdom. |
| 2011 | The Ambassador | Bought fake diplomatic passport ($130,000), posed as Liberian ambassador in Central African Republic, exposed BLOOD DIAMOND trade. |
| 2019 | Cold Case Hammarskjöld | Investigated UN Secretary-General's 1961 death. Went to South Africa. Found links to white supremacist org. WON SUNDANCE. |
| 2020 | The Mole | Infiltrated North Korean spy network in Denmark for 10 years. |
| 2024 | The Black Swan | Exposed Danish money laundering. 50% of Denmark watched. Led to arrests and new laws. |

**Legacy:** Danish Gonzo Publishing (1984) was named after Thompson's style. Mads Brügger is keeping it alive.

## The Assassinated

### 🇸🇪 Kim Wall (Sweden, 1987-2017)

**The Journalist Who Vanished at Sea**

Kim Wall was a rising star in international journalism.

**The Story:**
- Freelance journalist who wrote for NYT, Guardian, Vice, South China Morning Post
- Masters from Columbia University Journalism School
- Investigating Danish inventor Peter Madsen and his homemade submarine

**The End:**
August 10, 2017 — Went aboard Madsen's submarine. Never returned.

Her body was found:
- DECAPITATED
- Arms and legs cut off
- Weighted down with metal
- Washed ashore weeks later

Madsen claimed it was an "accident." He's serving life.

**Related:**
- [Peter R. de Vries](#-peter-r-de-vries-netherlands-1956-2021) — Also killed investigating crime

### 🇳🇱 Peter R. de Vries (Netherlands, 1956-2021)

**The Dutch Legend**

Peter R. de Vries was a household name in the Netherlands — a crime journalist who became a national hero.

**The Story:**
- Investigated organized crime for decades
- Key in solving the Freddy Heineken kidnapping (1983)
- Won an Emmy for his documentary on Natalee Holloway
- Advising witness Nabil B in trial against drug lord Ridouan Taghi

**The End:**
July 6, 2021 — Shot 5 times in Amsterdam. Died 9 days later.

He knew he was on a hit list. He continued anyway.

**Legacy:**
- Called "a national hero" by Amsterdam's mayor
- King Willem-Alexander expressed shock
- The attack was called "a crime against journalism" by EU President

**Related:**
- [Kim Wall](#-kim-wall-sweden-1987-2017) — Both killed, both investigating dangerous subjects

## Methodology

This archive documents journalists who embody the gonzo spirit:
- First-person immersive reporting
- Going undercover
- Becoming part of the story
- Risking freedom and life

Not all are "gonzo" in the strict Hunter S. Thompson sense, but all share the willingness to EMBED, INFILTRATE, and BECOME the story.
